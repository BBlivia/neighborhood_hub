Neighborhood-Hub

Neighborhood-Hub is a website that seeks to help connect those who are struggling with resources to help them in their time of need. This website seeks to simplify the lives of those who are struggling by making it easier to find the resources to help them. This website makes use of all the cutting-edge location tracking technologies in order to help people locate a way to what they need for support it could be a homeless shelter a homeless shelter a food bank or even a mental health clinic. The goal of the website is to integrate api technology in a way that helps those that are most in need like the homeless or those who are facing food insecurity. The reason why we chose to make it a website instead of an app is because not every one has acces to a phone espacially those in need so we thought that a website would be the best option for those we're trying to serve. This website is entirely ultristic and aims to help put a dent in the crissis that are going on in the country and have been worsened by the pandemic.
